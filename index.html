Welcome to TALC-sef

What is TALC-sef?

TALC-sef stands for pos-TAgged Literary Corpus, in Serbian, English and French. This page links to the associated github repository, in which you'll find pos-tagging models for Serbian, based on a 100,000 tokens manually revised reference corpus. Tagging models are available for BTagger, TnT and Treetagger. The best tagger is BTagger, but the optimal speed/accuracy compromise is TnT. Tagging models are for pos-tagging only: no lemmatization! Tags are based on a tagset aiming at a trade-off between accuracy and grammatical precision. In other words, it stands between the >900 tags from the CesANA corpus (G. Orwell's 1984 translation into Serbian), used in (Gesmundo and Samardzic, 2012), and Popovic's 16 tags tagsets.

Resources are provided for academic research. We've put a lot of effort into this, but, as always, glitches are inevitable here and there. We'll do our best to correct any errors in future versions.
